---
title: Production Deployment
description: Learn how to deploy your integration to production.
---

Before activating your production keys, you must complete the full KYB (Know Your Business) requirements. This process helps us understand your business and how you plan to use our payment APIs. The KYB process typically takes 1-2 business days.

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="KYB Process Timelines">
  - **Initial Review**: 1-2 business days
  - **Check Reservation feature**: Active upon initial review
  - **Wallet Acceptance feature**: Active upon initial review
  - **Card Acceptance feature**: Between 48 hours and 5 business days, as we validate your account information with external third parties.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
