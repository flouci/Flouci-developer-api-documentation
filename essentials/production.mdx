---
title: Production Deployment
description: Learn how to deploy your integration to production.
---

Once you have finished your tests on the **TEST APP**, you can simply create a new application via the developer API portal and change the `app_token` and `app_secret` in your environment variables.

<Info>
  **Note:** To create a production application, you need to have a Flouci Business wallet. Contact us, or a partner bank agency if you need support when creating the wallet.
</Info>
