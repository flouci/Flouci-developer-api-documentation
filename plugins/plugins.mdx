title: 'Plugins'
---

We offer powerful plugins for popular e-commerce platforms like WordPress (WooCommerce) and PrestaShop, designed to streamline your payment processing. Integrating Flouci into your online store is as simple as installing the appropriate plugin and configuring it with your account credentials.

 Once set up, you can begin accepting transactions immediately, with no complex coding or development required. This allows you to focus on your business while we handle the secure and reliable payment flow. 
 
 To get started, please email our support team at dev-support@flouci.com to request the plugin for your platform.
